Robot Store - Internal Notes and FAQs

Shipping Policy:
- Delivery takes 3 to 7 business days within Europe.
- Free shipping for orders above 500 EUR.
- International shipping available with additional fees.

Return Policy:
- Customers can return products within 30 days.
- Robots must be in original condition.
- Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

Warranty:
- All robots come with a 2-year warranty.
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects only.

FAQ:

Q: Can robots be controlled remotely?
A: Yes, most robots include a mobile app for remote control.

Q: Do robots require internet?
A: Some features require internet, but basic functions work offline.

Q: Are the robots safe for children?
A: Yes, especially the Educational Robot E1, which is designed for kids.

Q: Do you offer technical support?
A: Yes, 24/7 customer support is available.

Maintenance Notes:
- Clean sensors regularly for optimal performance.
- Keep software updated.
- Store robots in dry environments.
